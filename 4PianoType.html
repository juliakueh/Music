Piano Type

The piano is a manual keyboard instrument that creates sound by striking the strings with hammers. Using pedals or the amount of pressure applied to the keys can produce sound. Its wide range and chord-playing capability make it a universally loved musical instrument (Ferguson, 2022). The piano can serve as either a solo instrument or as a part of the orchestra.
