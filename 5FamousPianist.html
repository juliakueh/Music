Johann Sebastian Bach

(31 March 1685–28 July 1750)

A German organist and composer whosewith highly regarded mature Baroque music (Cartwright, 2023).
Famous Compositions:
1.The Goldberg Variations
2.The Well-Tempered Clavier
3.The Brandenburg Concertos
4.Concerto for Two Violins in D minor
source:(Pentreath, 2021).

Wolfgang Amadeus Mozart

(27 January 1756–5 December 1791)

Austrian composer who is regarded by many as one of the best in Western music history (Sadie, 2019).
Famous Compositions:
1.The Magic Flute, K620
2.Requiem Mass in D minor, K626
3.Symphony No.36 in C, K425 – Linz
4.Piano Concerto No.21 in C major, K467
source:(uDiscover Team, 2019).

Ludwig van Beethoven

(17 December 1770–26 March 1827)

German composer, pivotal in the transition from Classical to Romantic music (Julian Medforth Budden & Knapp, 2018).
Famous Compositions:
1.Fur Elise
2.Symphony No. 5
3.Fidelio, op.72
4.Symphony No.7 in A, op.92
source:(uDiscover Team, 2019).

Franz Schubert

(31 January 1797-19 November 1828)

Austrian composer of the late Classical and early Romantic eras (Maurice J.E. Brown, 2019).
Famous Compositions:
1.Ave Maria!
2.Das Wandern
3.Der Lindenbaum
4.Der Hirt auf dem Felsen
source:(Maurice J.E. Brown, 2019).

Frédéric Chopin

(1 March 1810-17 October 1849)

Austrian composer of the late Classical and early Romantic eras (Cikaj, 2023).
Famous Compositions:
1.Berceuse
2.Heroic Polonaise
3.Polonaise in G Minor
4.Chopin Preludes, Op. 28
source:(Hedley & Plantinga, 2019).

Robert Schumann

 

(8 June 1810-29 July 1856)


A famous German Romantic composer known for his piano compositions, lieder (songs), and orchestral works.
Famous Compositions:
1.Symphony No. 1, Op. 38
2.Symphony No. 3, Op. 97 (“Rhenish”)
3.Piano Concerto, Op. 54
4.Cello Concerto, Op. 129
source:(Review, 2019)

         

Franz Liszt

       

(22 October 1811–31 July 1886)

A highly influential and original Hungarian pianist and composer (Searle, 2018).
Famous Compositions:
1.overture
2.Apparitions
3.Faust Symphony
3.Bagatelle Without Tonality”
4.Hungarian Coronation Mass
source:(Searle, 2018).

      

Claude Debussy

(22 August 1862-25 March 1918)

A seminal French composer whose music greatly impacted the 20th century (Lockspeiser, 2018).
Famous Compositions:
1.Voiles
2.Nocturnes
3.Clair de lune
4. Pelléas et Mélisande
source:(Lockspeiser, 2018)
